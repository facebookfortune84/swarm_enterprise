PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

1. Data Sovereignty & Local Processing

Realms2Riches ("the Company," "we," "us") provides a "Programmable Company in a Box." A core tenet of our Swarm Enterprise OS is Data Sovereignty. Unlike traditional SaaS providers, all source code generated by the agentic swarm and all sensitive instruction data (the "Vibes") are processed on your private hardware.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you sign up, we collect your email address and encrypted credentials.
  • Transaction Data: Payment processing is handled via Stripe. We do not store your credit card information on our local factory servers.
  • System Metadata: We collect non-identifying telemetry (e.g., build success rates) to improve the 8-agent swarm logic, unless you opt-out via the .env configuration.

3. AI & Third-Party Brains

To operate, the swarm utilizes local inference (Ollama) and cloud planning (Groq). No proprietary code generated by the Lead Developer is stored or trained upon by Groq. We prioritize 100% Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) to ensure your company-in-a-box remains a private asset.

4. Liability Disclaimer for AI Output

The code produced by the autonomous agents is delivered "AS-IS." While our Security Overseer agent audits every file, we do not guarantee the absence of bugs or vulnerabilities. Final code review is the responsibility of the license holder.
